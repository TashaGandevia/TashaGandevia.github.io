Projects

Project Winter: Working Title

2024

Research Essay

Project Winter is a multiplayer survival game that innovatively combines traditional survival mechanics with social deception elements, creating a psychologically complex and emotionally charged experience. Set in a frozen wilderness, the game challenges players to navigate trust, betrayal, and communication under extreme pressure. Survivors must collaborate to achieve objectives and secure rescue, while traitors covertly sabotage these efforts, blurring the lines between allies and enemies. Key mechanics such as truth serum and mass hysteria amplify the dynamics of deception and mistrust, introducing layers of unpredictability that heighten the stakes of each interaction. Drawing on psychological and sociological theories of trust, deception, and group dynamics, this essay examines how Project Winter leverages its gameplay design to explore human behavior in high-stress, high-risk environments. By intertwining survival gameplay with the fragility of social trust, the game transcends traditional genre boundaries, offering players a unique and compelling exploration of interpersonal dynamics.
