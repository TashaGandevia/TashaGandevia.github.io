About Me

The person behind the projects

Family

My family is organized chaos — a husband, two kids, two dogs, and a cat who thinks he's in charge. The kids keep us on our toes, the dogs alternate between protectors and snack thieves, and the cat is a world-class napper who tolerates us as long as the food keeps coming. Every day's an adventure in our kid-powered, pet-filled circus!

Passions

My life is a bit of a triathlon — swap cycling for paddleboarding, add family gaming marathons, and throw in a crafting session or two. With 30+ running medals from 5Ks to half marathons, I'm always one good race away from a podium…or a nice nap. When I'm not moving, I'm trying new crafts — usually ending up with more glue on me than on the project.

Escape Room Enthusiast

My husband and I are escape room enthusiasts (some say addicts), having cleared every room in the Lower Mainland and Whistler, plus a few in Seattle. With a 90%+ success rate, we like to think we're pros — though we occasionally bring the kids for "hard mode." Nothing says teamwork like convincing a 10-year-old not to press every button they see!
